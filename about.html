About Kite Charity Organization

Welcome to the KITE Charity Foundation , where every hand extended is a life uplifted. We're dedicated to bringing positive change to underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and empowerment programs. Join us in making a difference.

KITE Charity Foundation is a hope giving foundation that advocates and goes out of their way and comfort to see a smile on the faces of the disadvantaged be it children or adults for we believe no candle loses it’s light by lighting another.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be a powerful voice for change, where we advocate for equitable access to healthcare and education, and raise awareness about the importance of these fundamental rights. Our mission is to actively engage communities, policymakers, and stakeholders to drive policy reforms, challenge inequalities, and foster a culture of inclusivity, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to thrive through improved health and education.

Why Kite Charity Foundation?

KITE Charity Foundation is one that is driven and fueled by passion to help and put a smile on the faces of the disadvantaged as the members have since 2020 tried to offer help to others with their own resources but because of lack of adequate resources to cause a far reaching positive impact they have come together to make their selfless beings officially recognized in order to create a pool of resources and see to it that they create the far reaching positive impact in the world.

Our Goals

We are committed to achieving the following objectives:

1. Providing Aid and Support

Offering financial assistance, food, shelter, and medical services to those in need.

2. Promoting Education and Literacy

Empowering communities through education and literacy programs.

3. Advocating for Marginalized Groups

Protecting the rights of marginalized groups and advocating for their inclusion.

4. Advocating for Girl Child Education

Promoting girl child education and ensuring access to basic needs for girls in the community.

5. Supporting Research and Development

Investing in research and development in areas such as health, environment, and technology.

6. Encouraging Volunteerism

Fostering a spirit of giving and cooperation through volunteerism and community involvement.
